Facilities

  Smart Campus


Located within a lush green locality of Pithampur in Indore, Indore Institute of Science & Technology (IIST) presents a perfect environ for learning. The institute possesses a sprawling campus which is powered by Educational Resource Planning (ERP) software which maximizes its potential and helps in optimizing its resources in the best possible manner.

  Library


IIST Indore boasts of a technical library which is spread in a whopping 15,000 sq ft. The library has a vast collection of business magazines, pharmacy books, national and international journals and periodicals which prove to be of immense value to the students. The library also offers book bank facility to all the students which allows each student to issue six books for a semester. Moreover, students can also issue additional books if refereed by the faculty.

  Auditoriums and Seminar Hall


IIST has two auditoriums which are spread over an area of 11,500 sq ft. and are laced with latest facilities. In addition there is also a four stepped seminar hall that is used regularly and frequently by students.
These are venues that have been witness to some of the electrifying performances which different student groups have conjured. They figure in the memories of students even though they might have finished with their journey with IIST.
No wonder than that they are amongst one of the most important part of IIST and the one which are looked after with great care and dedication.

  Sports


We understand the value of sports in overall development of human mind and body and as such provide some of the very best sporting facilities to our students. Apart from such popular outdoor sports like cricket, hockey, football, table tennis etc. IIST also has started yoga and meditation classes which help students in recuperating mentally.

  Canteen


Multi-cuisine spacious canteen caters to the taste buds of students at an economical rate.

  Hostel


IIST provides top class hostel facilities to its students. It has separate arrangement for boys and girls with both the hostels having a capacity of more than 250 students. Each room is shared by three students and is equipped with modern amenities as well as attached bath and toilet.
